Sragen (ꦱꦿꦒꦺꦤ꧀)

Sragen (ꦱꦿꦒꦺꦤ꧀) is a district in the eastern part of Central Java (Jawa Tengah) province in Indonesia. Its capital is the town of Sragen, located about 30km to the northeast of Surakarta. Sragen borders Grobogan in the North, Ngawi in the East, Karanganyar in the South, and Boyolali in the West. Sragen is also known as "Bumi Sukowati". Even though the area is small, only around 994.57km2. Sragen itself is one of the highest rice producers in Central Java, even Indonesia.
